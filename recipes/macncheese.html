Mac N Cheese Recipe

Description

This is one of the first recipes that I tried out from the Instapot cook book that Nicole got me for Xmas 2022.

My first attempt went ok. The recipe calls for an absurd amount of cheese. I added half of what was supposed to be included and it was still insanely greasy. So that's something I'd note for the future.

Ingredients

  • Mac
  • Cheese
  • Oil
  • Milk
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • Additional cheese
  1. Put the oil and milk in the instapot
  2. Add in the chicken broth. Remember 1 teaspoon of concentrated boullion in 1 cup of water is equivalent to 1 cup of broth.
  3. Pressure cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the grated cheese until your arms are ready to fall off.
